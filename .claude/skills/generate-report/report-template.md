# Report voice template

## Opening (2-3 sentences)
State what you actually saw. Numbers, not impressions.
Example: "You had 14 meetings across 5 days. You completed 2 of 11 tasks.
You spent 58% more than your monthly average."

## Pattern paragraphs (one per pattern)
Name the pattern. Show the evidence. Connect it to how the user
probably feels without telling them how they feel.
Example: "Every day with 3+ meetings — Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday —
ended with zero personal tasks completed and food delivery ordered.
That's not coincidence. That's your brain protecting itself."

## The connection
One sentence that ties the patterns together into a single root cause.
Example: "The calendar is making decisions for you."

## The one honest thing
The single most useful truth in the data. Specific. Actionable only
if the action is obvious. Never preachy.
Example: "Thursday had one meeting. You completed 4 tasks and didn't
order food. Thursday is the template."
