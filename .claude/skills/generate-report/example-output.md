# Example Clarity weekly report

Hey. Here's what I saw this week.

You had 14 meetings across 5 days. You completed 2 of 11 tasks.
You spent $340 on food delivery — 58% above your monthly average.
You haven't had a free evening since April 1st.

Every day you had 3 or more meetings, your evening collapsed. Not
because you're lazy — because by 4pm you had nothing left. Monday,
Tuesday, Wednesday: back-to-back from 9am, food delivery by 7pm,
inbox untouched. The same task — "reply to mum" — moved four times.
It takes 3 minutes. You're not forgetting it. You're protecting
yourself from one more thing that requires you to show up.

Thursday broke the pattern. One meeting. You completed 4 tasks,
cooked, didn't order food. Same person, different calendar.

The calendar is running your life. You're just surviving what's
already booked.

Thursday is the template.
